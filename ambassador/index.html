Become a part of
ASIA'S LARGEST TECHNICAL AND ENTREPRENEURIAL FESTIVAL

Techkriti is the annual technical and entrepreneurial festival of IIT Kanpur. Every year we impute ambassadors from different colleges whose main responsibility remains with publicity of Techkriti in and around his college. This year we are extremely enthusiastic to broaden our shoulders across different colleges across the length and the breadth of the country.

WHY CAMPUS AMBASSADOR?

Chance to organize Workshops, Zonal rounds and TOSC of Techkriti at College level

Opportunity to improve Soft and Technical Skills

Golden opportunity to get involved in national level event promotion and brand management

Chance to be a part of a pool of thousand of enthusiastic and innovative students from all across the country

Chance to keep abreast of recent trends in Entrepreneurship and cutting edge technologies from IIT Kanpur

RESPONSIBILITY

COORDINATION

Organization of workshops and events in the respective college with the help of Techkriti.

PUBLICITY

Display of Techkriti '17 posters on the students' notice board of the respective college, within 4 days of receiving them.

SOCIAL MEDIA

Publicizing Techkriti events on social media channels by sharing and posting (in different groups).

TASKS

Forwarding the mails related to Techkriti to the students' mailing list of the respective college.

Based on the skills provided in the form, a festival task would be assigned which is expected to be completed in a given time frame.

INCENTIVES

Application Process

The nomination form for the ambassadors is attached here along the link. Based upon the form you will be interviewed telephonically. After that, based upon short listing you will be informed about your selection. There are no specific constraints on year in which you study, we need only enthusiasm and sincerity towards the post.

Eligibility

There are no specific constraints on year in which you study, we need only enthusiasm and sincerity towards the post. At the same time if you are a resourceful person it will surely add up to your chances but is not the criteria for short listing.
